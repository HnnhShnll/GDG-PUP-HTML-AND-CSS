Top 3 Things I Need for My Hobby

  • Badminton Equipment (Badminton Racket, Shuttlecock, Badminton Shoes, and Sportswear)
  • Badminton Court
  • Water

Steps to Get Started

  1. Purchase badminton equipment that is suitable for you.
  2. Find and rent a badminton court where you can play.
  3. Do warm-ups and dynamic stretches before playing badminton to prepare your body and prevent injuries.
  4. Familiarize yourself with the court and the court rules.
  5. Learn and practice the basic techniques and skills of playing badminton.
  6. Find a playmate and apply all the techniques and skills that you have learned.
  7. Do not forget to stay hydrated!
